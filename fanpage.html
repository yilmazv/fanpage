Curtis James Jackson III also known as 50 Cent is an American born rapper, actor and an entrepreneur. He is best known for his huge impact in the hip hop industry and has solid over 30 million albums. Hes currently only 46 but has been through a roller coaster of events within his life. Ranging from being shot 9 times to going to jail. Currently hes slowly stepping away from the music industry and slowly migrating towards production of movies and TV shows. He is currently directing the hit TV show power and many other films and shows.

His most well known movies that he has stared in were Den of Thieves and also his own film about his life Get rich or Die Tryin'. The most recent appearence of him was for Super Bowl LVI where him and a few other artists hosted the half time show.
